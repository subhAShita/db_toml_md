<details><summary>English</summary>

Simultaneously evoking love and fear in the mind of the enemy while stretching the bow with the combined sounds of the bracelet and the bowstring, Candi's arm reigns supreme.
</details>

चापाकर्षणलीलामिलद्वलयशिञ्जिनीक्वाणः ॥
