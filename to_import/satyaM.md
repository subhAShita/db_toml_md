<details><summary>Text</summary>

सत्यं सुष्ठु विजानन्तोऽप्यसत्यं ब्रुवते जनाः ।  
मनोजिह्वान्तरे किं तत्सर्वं येनान्यथाकृतम् ॥
</details>

<details><summary>English</summary>

Though they know the truth very well, people speak falsehood. What exists between the mind and the tongue that has altered the very definition of things?
</details>
