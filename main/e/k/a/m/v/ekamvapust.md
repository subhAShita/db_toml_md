<details open><summary>Text</summary>

एकं वपुस्तच्छिवयोस्त्रिलोकी-
द्वैराज्यसिंहासनकान्ति वोऽव्यात् ।  
छत्रायते यत्र शिरोऽभ्रगङ्गा-  
वीचीविपर्यस्तवपुर्मृगाङ्कः ॥
</details>



<details><summary>English</summary>

May that single form occupied both by Shiva and Parvati, appearing, therefore, like a lion-throne in the diarchial kingdom of the three worlds (i.e., ruled simultaneously by Shiva and Parvati, and therefore having two rulers) protect us. The crescent moon, turved topsy-turvy by the wave of the celestial Ganga on the crest, looks like the white parasol adorning that throne.
</details>
