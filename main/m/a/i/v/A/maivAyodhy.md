<details open><summary>Text</summary>

नैवायोध्यां गमिष्यामो  
गमिष्यामो न दण्डकान्।  
कुशलं भरतस्यास्तु  
रामस्यास्तु तथा सुखम्।।
</details>



<details><summary>English</summary>

People of Ayodhya who are treated with festivities in the āśrama of Bharadvāja declare that they’d stay there itself, they’d neither go to Ayodhya nor Daṇḍaka and wish both Rāma and Bharata prosperity and happiness.
</details>
