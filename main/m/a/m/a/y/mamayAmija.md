<details open><summary>Text</summary>

न मया निजपारम्य-  
स्थिरा देवि त्वमर्चिता ।  
नमयानि जपारम्य-  
द्युते मौलिं क्षमस्व माम् ॥
</details>



<details><summary>विश्वास-टिप्पनी</summary>

न मया निज-पारम्य-  
स्थिरा देवि त्वम् अर्चिता ।  
**नमयानि** जपा-रम्य-  
द्युते **मौलिं** क्षमस्व माम् ॥
</details>



<details><summary>English</summary>

O Devi, who resembles the hibiscus flower by her red glow! I have not worshipped you, who are firmly established in your supremacy. May I (therefore) bend my head before you. Do forgive me.
</details>
