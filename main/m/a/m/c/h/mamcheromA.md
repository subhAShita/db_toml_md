<details open><summary>Text</summary>

मञ्चे रोमाञ्चिताङ्गी रतिमृदिततनोः कर्कटीवाटिकायां  
कान्तस्याङ्गे प्रमोदादुभयभुजपरिष्वक्तकण्ठे निलीना ।  
पादेन प्रेङ्खयन्ती मुखरयति मुहुः पामरी फेरवाणां  
रात्रावुत्त्रासहेतोर्वृतिशिखरलतालम्बिनीं कम्बुमालाम् ॥
</details>



<details><summary>अज्ञात-विवरणम्</summary>

At night, a rustic woman is sleeping happily on her  
bed in a cucumber field. Covered in goosebumps,  
she puts her arms around the neck of her lover,  
exhausted after lovemaking. And with her foot,  
she repeatedly shakes a string of conch shells  
hung from a creeper atop the the bordering fence in order to drive away hungry jackals with its resounding jingle.
</details>
