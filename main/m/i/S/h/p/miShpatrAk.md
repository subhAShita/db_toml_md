<details><summary>Text</summary>

निष्पत्त्रा क्रियमाणा त्रैलोक्योद्यानिका लुलायेन ।  
उद्यम्य चरणदण्डं यया ररक्षे नमामि तां चण्डीम् ॥
</details>



<details><summary>English</summary>

I bow to Chandi, who safeguarded the garden called the world by brandishing the staff that is her leg, when it was afflicted/stripped-of-leaves by the buffalo-demon.
</details>
