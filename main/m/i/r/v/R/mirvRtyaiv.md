<details open><summary>Text</summary>

निर्वृत्यै वोऽस्तु नित्यं प्रमथपरिवृढो दुर्निवारान्धकार-  
प्राग्भारोन्मेषमीलन्मलिनगलतया यो युगापायनृत्ये ।  
स्कन्धालम्बानपेक्षं पृथगिव चलता सान्ध्यकालाभ्रबभ्रु-  
भ्रूभेदोग्रेण कामप्यधिवसति दशां पञ्चकेनाननानाम् ॥
</details>



<details><summary>अज्ञात-विवरणम्</summary>

While he is dancing at the end of an eon, his dark throat disappears in the dense darkness that surrounds him. And it seems his five faces, formidable with brows tawny like evening clouds, are moving apparently without being supported on the trunk. May he, Shiva, bring us joy.
</details>
