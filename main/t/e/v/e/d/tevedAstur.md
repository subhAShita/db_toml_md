<details open><summary>Text</summary>

ते वेदास्तुरगास्तदीयकविता यन्ता शरस्तत्पिता  
तन्नेत्रे च रथस्य चक्रयुगलं चापं तदावृत्तिभूः ।  
तद्वज्ज्या क्षितिधारणे पटुरिति प्लोषे पुराणां मिथः-  
सम्बद्धैर्विलसन्निजोपकरणैस्त्र्यक्षोऽस्तु वस्तुष्टये ॥
</details>



<details><summary>अज्ञात-विवरणम्</summary>

The four Vedas became the horses; Composer of the Vedas - Brahma - became the charioteer; Brahma's father - Vishnu - became the arrow; Vishnu's eyes - the sun and moon - became the wheels The path around which the sun and moon revolve - Meru - became the bow; that which share's Meru's burden of bearing the earth - Adishesha - became the bow-string  
In this way, he used mutually related means to burn down Tripuras  
May he - Tryaksha - grant us bliss
</details>
