<details open><summary>Text</summary>

विचेष्टितानां खलसज्जनानां  
प्रदर्श्य नाट्यं सफलं ततोऽन्ते।  
रस-प्रवृद्धिं शुभकृत् सुनामा   
दधातु संवत्सरसूत्रधारी॥
</details>



<details><summary>English</summary>

Having shown the drama of antics of bad and good people - with their fruits/ fruitfully, may the stagemanager who is the aptly named year "ShubhakRt" bear forth in the end a great frothing forth of aesthetic juices.
</details>
