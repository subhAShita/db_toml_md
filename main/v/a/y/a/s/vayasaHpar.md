<details><summary>Text</summary>

वयसः परिणामे ऽपि यः खलः खल एव सः ।  
सुपक्वमपि माधुर्यं नोपयातीन्द्रवारुणम् ॥
</details>



<details><summary>English - Adheesh</summary>

A scoundrel is still a scoundrel,  
Even after the effects of age.  
A bitter gourd, even when fully ripe,  
Won't ever turn sweet.
</details>
