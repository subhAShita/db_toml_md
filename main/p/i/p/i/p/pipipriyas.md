<details open><summary>Text</summary>

पिपिप्रिय ससस्वयं मुमुमुखासवं देहि मे ततत्यज दुदुद्रुतं भभभभाजनं काञ्चनम्।  
इति स्खलितजल्पितं मदवशात्कुरङ्गीदृशः प्रगे हसितहेतवे सहचरीभिरध्येयत॥
</details>



<details><summary>शङ्करः</summary>

"De de dear, let me drink the wi wi wine from your mou mou mouth directly. Le le let go the go go golden go go goblet" - these were the sweet prattles of the doe-eyed beauty when she was drunk the previous night. And at dawn, her friends had a hearty laugh by imitating them.
</details>
