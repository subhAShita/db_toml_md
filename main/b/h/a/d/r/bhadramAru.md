<details open><summary>Text</summary>

भद्रं मारुत हव्यवाह कुशलं दृश्यध्व आपश्चिराद्  
उर्वि स्वागतमस्तु ते गगन भो दिष्ट्यासि कर्णातिथिः ।   
भूतानामिति यत्र कल्पविगमे सुप्त्वोत्थितानां मिथः  
सर्गादौ सुखपृच्छिका प्रचलति प्रीत्यै स वः स्याच्छिवः ॥
</details>



<details><summary>English</summary>

"Wind! Hope you are doing good" "Fire! All well?" "Waters! We see you after a long time" "Earth! A grand welcome to you" "Space! By God's grace, we hear you once again" - In this way, the five Elements, which had gone to sleep within him during the great apocalypse, enquire about each other's welfare when they wake up again during the beginning of a new creation. May he, SthANu, confer happiness on us.
</details>
