<details open><summary>Text</summary>

भ्रातर्वारिद वर्ष किन्तु न तथा सा नाभिसर्ता यथा  
तां पश्यामि यथा तथा ज्वल सखे मा दीप जिह्रेतु सा ।  
वाताः शीकरिणस्तथा वहत भो धत्ते यथा प्रेयसी  
मद्दोर्बन्धरसामृतप्रणयिनीमङ्गेषु रोमोद्गतिम् ॥
</details>



<details><summary>English</summary>

Dear Cloud! Do rain, but not so much she does not come to meet me. Lamp, my friend! Do shine, so I can see her, but let her not be overcome with shyness. O winds, that carry water droplets! Please blow around but let my beloved's hair brizzle due to the nectarine embrace of my arms. (inspiration from a Kannada movie song)
</details>
