<details><summary>Text</summary>

अर्भकेष्टपरिपूर्तये मुदा  
ब्रह्मदत्तवरपालनाय सः ।  
जम्भजाधवमदेभकेसरी  
स्तम्भजोऽभवदनादिमान्हरिः ।।
</details>



<details><summary>English</summary>

To fulfill the wishes of a child,  
To ensure that the boon of Brahma was followed,    
The lion to mad elephant-husband of the daughter of Jambha (Hiranyakashipu),
The one without a beginning,  
Hari (Lord Narasimha), was happily *born* in a pillar,
</details>
