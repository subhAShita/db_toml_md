<details open><summary>Text</summary>

अव्याद्विष्णोरनघमघवग्रावनीलोऽङ्घ्रिरस्मा-  
न्यत्संवाहक्रमरसजुषः पाणिरम्भोधिपुत्र्याः ।  
यातायातै रणितवलयोऽभ्यर्णगङ्गातरङ्ग-  
त्वङ्गद्भृङ्गावलिमुखरिताम्भोजशङ्कां विधत्ते ॥
</details>



<details><summary>अज्ञात-विवरणम्</summary>

Lakshmi's hand - it bangles jingling - moves to and fro over Vishnu's foot as she intently massages it. This hand appears like a lotus - abuzz with bees - swaying from side to side in a wave of River Ganga, arising from that very foot. May that foot - dark like sapphire - save us
</details>
