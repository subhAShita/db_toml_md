<details open><summary>Text</summary>

शुक्तौ भवन्ति मणयो यदिति प्रसिद्धम्  
मन्ये ऋतं न वचनं खलु पण्डितानाम्।  
स्प्रष्टुं क्षमा ननु सरित्पतिरोधसि त्वाम्  
ते बिन्दवो हि भुवि मौक्तिकतां भजन्ते॥
</details>



<details><summary>अज्ञात-विवरणम्</summary>

Pearls are born in oysters' say the learned. But this famous fact, I know, is far from truth. Sweetheart! Those little drops from the ocean that are fortunate enough to touch your limbs when you are standing on the shore, I think, turn into pearls later.
</details>
