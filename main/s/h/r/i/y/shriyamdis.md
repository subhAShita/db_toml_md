<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

श्रियं दिशतु वः शश्वन्  
नाभ्य्-अम्भो-रुहं हरेः ।  
यस्मिन्न् ओंकार-झङ्कारी  
विधिर् मधुकरायते ॥ ४.१ ॥
</details>



<details><summary>Text</summary>

श्रियं दिशतु वः शश्वन्नाभ्यम्भोरुहं हरेः ।  
यस्मिन्नोंकारझङ्कारी विधिर्मधुकरायते ॥ ४.१ ॥
</details>



<details><summary>English - Adheesh</summary>

May the lotus that is growing in the navel of Hari  
Always give you blessings—  
Lord Brahmā the Creator is there inside it,  
Acting like a honeybee,  
    As he hums the sacred syllable ॐ.
</details>
