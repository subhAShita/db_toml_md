<details><summary>Text</summary>

“श्रावं श्रावमरोचकीमुपगताः कर्णाः स्वरैस्सप्तभि  
र्यामस्ते किल यात एव; शिरसा प्रत्यग्रतां कुर्महे ।  
मुञ्चत्येष बको ऽष्टमं, नवमसौ घूकः, तदभ्यस्यता”-  
मित्येषा पिक! तेऽपि सेत्स्यति दशा, सिद्धा न चेत्साम्प्रतम् ॥
</details>



<details><summary>Suhas - English</summary>

“Our ears are tired of hearing
these same seven notes.
You’re out of date
We like novelty.

The crane knows an eighth note
and the owl a ninth.
Learn from them.”

Cuckoo, you too shall
soon embrace this fate
if it has not embraced you already.
</details>
