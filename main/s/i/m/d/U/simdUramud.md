<details open><summary>Text</summary>

सिन्दूरमुद्रितो जीयाद्गणेशस्योत्थितः करः ।  
वियद्बर्हातपत्रस्य वहन्विद्रुमदण्डताम् ॥
</details>



<details><summary>English</summary>

May Ganesha's raised trunk, reddened by the application of sindUra, reign supreme. It appears like a coral staff of the peacock-feather umbrella that is the sky.
</details>
