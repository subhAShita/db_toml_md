<details open><summary>Text</summary>

स्नेहस्य तारतम्येन दूरोऽन्यो निकटोऽथवा ।  
नान्तरं मीयते हस्तैर्न क्रोशैर्न च योजनैः ॥
</details>



<details><summary>राजारामज-शङ्करः</summary>

The distance between two people is measured in terms of mutual love, not in centimeters, meters, or kilometers.
</details>
