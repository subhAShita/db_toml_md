<details open><summary>Text</summary>

उन्मिषन्निमिषन्नानाजगत्कमलकैरवा ।  
जीयात्संविदुषोलक्ष्मीरुद्यद्द्युमणिपाटला ॥
</details>



<details><summary>विश्वास-टिप्पनी</summary>

उन्मिषन्-निमिषन्-नाना-  
जगत्-कमल-कैरवा+++(=कुमुदपुष्पा)+++ ।  
जीयात् संविद्-उषो-लक्ष्मीर्  
उद्यद्-द्यु-मणि-पाटला ॥
</details>



<details><summary>English</summary>

Hail Shakti - Supreme Consciousness. Akin to the rising sun in her redness (reddened by the rising sun), she is the beautiful dawn in which universes bloom and fade away like lotuses and lilies (respectively)
</details>
