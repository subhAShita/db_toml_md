<details open><summary>Text</summary>

दैत्येन्द्रायतन-स्थूणा-  
पञ्जरावास-निःसहः ।  
हरिर् जीयान् नखाग्रेषु  
क्षिपन् दृष्टिं मुहुर्मुहुः ॥
</details>



<details><summary>English</summary>

May Hari (Vishnu/Lion/Vishnu as नरहरि) who, annoyed by  his (uncomfortable) stay in the cage, namely, the pillar in Hiranyakashipu's palace, repeatedly let his eyes fall on his sharp nails, reign supreme.
</details>
