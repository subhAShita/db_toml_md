<details open><summary>Text</summary>

दानवारिमदोन्मत्ता  
गजाः सन्ति वने वने।  
बलिमेव द्विपं मन्ये  
यो नष्टो दानवारिणा॥
</details>



<details><summary>English</summary>

Shlesha 1 - दानव+अरि and दान-वारि.  
arthashleSha - You can also interpret it as one who was subdued by the waters used to sanctify the gift(daana).
</details>
