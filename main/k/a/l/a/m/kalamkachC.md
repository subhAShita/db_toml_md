<details><summary>Text</summary>

कलङ्कच्छद्माविष्कृतमलिनगूढाक्षरलिपिं   
पुरस्ताद्बिभ्राणा सकलशशिगल्वर्कगुलिकाम् ।  
इयं ब्रूते चीरीध्वनिभिरधुना सिद्धशबरी  
निशा पान्थस्त्रीणां बहलकरुणं भावि मरणम् ॥
</details>



<details><summary>English</summary>

Like a gypsy woman with magical powers, the Night holds in front of her the crystal ball that is the full moon,  
its black mark appearing like some sad, mysterious message,  
and proclaims the pitiable death of lovelorn women through the chirp of crickets
</details>
